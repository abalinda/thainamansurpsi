Specialized therapy for Brazilians living abroad

Living abroad can be a challenging experience, bringing feelings of loneliness, anxiety, and adaptation difficulties. I offer online therapy for Brazilians abroad, providing a safe and welcoming space to deal with issues such as stress, homesickness, and cultural challenges. As a Brazilian psychologist, I understand the particularities of expatriate life and help you find your balance, wherever you are.
