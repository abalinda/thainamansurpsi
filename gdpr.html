Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26/11/2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when using our website. We comply with the General Data Protection Law (LGPD - Law No. 13.709/2018) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data Collection

When accessing our website, we may collect some information automatically, including:

  • IP Address
  • Browser and device type
  • Pages visited and browsing time

If you contact us via form, WhatsApp, or email, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

2. Data Usage

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Respond to your messages and schedule appointments.
  • Improve user experience on the site through traffic analysis (Google Analytics or similar tools).
  • Ensure the security and proper functioning of the website.

We do not share, sell, or use your data for purposes not specified in this policy.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies to improve your experience, such as storing language preferences and collecting access statistics. You can disable cookies in your browser at any time.

4. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data stored by us.
  • Request information about the use of your data.
  • Revoke your consent for the use of your data at any time.

If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at contato@thainamansur.com.

5. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or improper disclosure.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We recommend that you review this page regularly to be aware of any changes.

If you have questions, please contact us at contato@thainamansur.com.
